awards
GOOGLE CODE-IN GRAND PRIZE WINNER • Apertium • JAN 2018
I became a winner of international competition organized by Google. For over two months, I was working for open source organization called Apertium - a free/open-source rule-based machine translation platform. Because of this accomplishment, I was invited to Google Headquarters in Mountain View, California.
HACKATHON WINNER • Hack Heroes • DEC 2018
I was named a winner of Polish hackathon about public data - Hack Heroes. In order to achieve it, I developed web application for finding free parking spots based on publicly available CCTV footage around the city. I used TensorFlow and Keras to create and train deep learning model on my own data.
HACKATHON NCR PRIZE • Hack The North • SEP 2018
Using React Native, I developed iOS/Android application to suggest food based on which nutrients one is lacking and exceeding in as per the daily quotas. You can check out my Medium article about Hack The North here.
