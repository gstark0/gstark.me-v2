about me
My name is Gregory Stark, I'm 19 years old freelancer, software developer and machine learning researcher currently living in Poland. I'm especially fascinated by artificial intelligence and deep learning but I do love all the latest trends in technology.

Every single day I try my best to be productive, develop my skills and constantly chase my dreams. Apart from that, I'm addicted to coding.

awards
GOOGLE CODE-IN GRAND PRIZE WINNER • Apertium • JAN 2018
I became a winner of international competition organized by Google. For over two months, I was working for open source organization called Apertium - a free/open-source rule-based machine translation platform. Because my hard work, I was invited to Google Headquarters in Mountain View, California.
HACKATHON WINNER • Hack Heroes • DEC 2018
I was named a winner of Polish hackathon about public data - Hack Heroes. In order to achieve it, I developed web application to search for free parking spaces based on publicly available CCTV footage around the city. I used TensorFlow and Keras to create and train deep learning model on my own data.
HACKATHON NCR PRIZE • Hack The North • SEP 2018
Using React Native, I developed iOS/Android application to suggest food based on which nutrients one is lacking and exceeding in as per the daily quotas. You can check out my Medium article about Hack the North experience here.
